HealthOptima

Your Personal Food & Health Advisor.

Make confident food choices tailored to your health. With ingredient recognition, nutrition details, and health analytics, HealthOptima guides you through a smarter, healthier lifestyle.

Key Features

  • ✅ Upload image of food/ingredients
  • ✅ OCR extracts text using EasyOCR
  • ✅ Analyze based on user’s selected health conditions
  • ✅ CSV-based smart logic for food suitability
  • ✅ Search food by name to get detailed nutrition charts
  • ✅ Analyze your health conditions (BMI, BMR, TDEE, body fat) with suggestions and visual comparisons
  • ✅ Access related health videos and follow trusted health creators

Who It's For

  • 👨‍🎓 Students with bad eating habits
  • 👩‍⚕️ People with diabetes, allergies, or other health conditions
  • 👯‍♂️ Anyone who trusts food more than friends
  • 🍜 Basically, anyone who's ever wondered “What’s really in this?”

Technologies Used

  • 🐍 Python (Backend + OCR)
  • 📄 CSV Data for logic
  • 🤖 EasyOCR
  • 🌐 HTML + CSS + Flask
